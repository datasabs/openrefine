Preparing for the workshop

Install software tools

  • OpenRefine
    • Download and install OpenRefine
    • If you’re on Windows and don’t already have Java installed, select the Windows kit with embedded Java
    • Take 5 minutes to watch this introductory video for a preview of what OpenRefine can do


  • 7zip (optional)
    • If you don’t already have a utility for unzipping compressed files, 7zip is both free and useful.
    • Download and install 7zip

Create a free ICPSR account

You’ll need an ICPSR account to download data for the workshop. ICPSR is a data repository maintained by the Inter-university Consortium for Political and Social Research at the University of Michigan.

  1. Go to the ICPSR website
  2. Click the Log In button in the upper right
  3. Scroll down and click the blue “Create Account” button
  4. Use your Duke email address to create your new account
    • You must use your Duke email to tie your account to Duke’s ICPSR membership. If you use a non-Duke email, you may encounter issues.
  5. Select “Yes” to allow the OR on your campus to view your information
  6. Save your log-in information somewhere you can easily find it again; you’ll need it at the beginning of the workshop
